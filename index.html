what it does

InfinityCharts, wherever you go

VFR

Worldwide VFR charts

Global charts rendered from the sim data, always accurate and up-to-date with the simulation.

IFR

IFR charts

Dedicated IFR maps, procedures, approach and departure plates, missed approach information, holds, MSA display, and terrain-aware planning.

MAP

Multiple styles per location

Switch between ICAO, Sectional, TPC, CAA and other chart styles for any location.

NAV

Search and facilities

Airport, VOR, NDB, FIX, airspace, and facility lookup ordered by proximity. Jump to any location with a click.

FPL

Fast route planning

Click route legs to add waypoints, drag them into place, view distance and magnetic course per leg, then import, export, paste, reverse, or reorder the plan.

TLS

Navigation tools

Built-in number cruncher, analog chronograph, calculator watch, analog flight computer, drawing, eraser, undo, flight plan import/export, and physical rulers.

Video workflow

Real-world VFR, in the sim

Plan along geography and landmarks

Build a route in seconds. The elevation profile shows terrain along the route plus the highest elevation, MEF of the quadrant, and MORA. Leg ticks can be time-based or distance-based for dead reckoning.

Measure like paper

Rotate the chart, pull out the ruler, read radial and nautical-mile distance, draw notes directly on the map, and cross out segments as you fly.

Fly without the magenta line

Hide the GPS aircraft position, navigate from rivers, lakes, villages, cliffs, wind drift, and elapsed time. Switch between true and magnetic headings when needed.

Keep the work

Save and load routes plus map artwork so long trips, hand-drawn notes, and visual checkpoints stay preserved between flights.
